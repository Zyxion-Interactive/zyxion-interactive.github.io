Signal Lost: Protocol

About the Project

Signal Lost: Protocol is a narrative stealth-survival game that puts you in the shoes of an agent investigating the aftermath of a failed contact with an unknown entity. What starts as a routine response unravels into a deeper mystery involving classified technologies, government experiments, and extraterrestrial forces.

The game features immersive environments, puzzles, stealth mechanics, and resource management to deliver a tense and atmospheric experience. Inspired by titles like SCP, Alien Isolation, and Deus Ex, it focuses on immersion, fear, and storytelling.

Signal Lost: Protocol is being developed for PC in Unreal Engine (singleplayer/co-op).
